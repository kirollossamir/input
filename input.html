    Monitor

    A monitor is a peripheral device which displays computer output on a screen. Screen output is referred to as a soft copy

    Types of Monitors

    Cathode-ray tube (CRT):

    Resemble televisions; use picture tube technology; inexpensive, but they take up desk space and use a lot of energy.

    Liquid Crystal Display (LCD or flat-panel):

    Cells sandwiched between two transparent layers form images; used for notebook computers, PDAs, cellular phones, and personal computers; expensive, and they take up less desk space and use less energy than CRT monitors
